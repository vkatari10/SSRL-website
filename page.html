LEARNSat

Low-Earth Acessible Research Network Satellite

A new venture developed by the UGA SSRL giving high-school students opportunites to work with CubeSat technology

Purpose: Overcome hurdles of cost and technical requiements to entering CubeSat research

This program allows high-school students to create payloads housed in satellite payloads developed by the UGA SSRL given to students as LEARNSat kits consisitng of satelitte bus and education content required to assemble it

Launched using weather balloons, this suboritmal mission will reduce exorbitant launch costs as well as reducing compatability with NASA's stringent tecnical requirements, making the process easier for high school students.

Competition

$2000 given to high schools students to come up with a research proposal and the best compeitotr gets 2000 to actually develop their own ideas!
